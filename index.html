At IIT Mandi's Center for AI & Robotics Lab, I work on reinforcement learning-based control for robotic manipulators, bridging the gap between simulation and real hardware.

  • Developed RL environments for 6-DOF manipulators in Isaac Lab, addressing non-spherical wrist kinematics where standard IK decoupling is computationally complex.
  • Implemented joint velocity control via PPO with energy-aware reward functions to minimize oscillations and ensure smooth transitions for physical hardware.
  • Engineered a Sim-to-Real pipeline analyzing simulation frequencies and contact dynamics on policy convergence and stability.
  • Formulated Dual-Arm Cooperative IK using MAPPO, enforcing relative pose and antipodal constraints for synchronized end-effector alignment.
  • Integrated safety-critical constraints — joint limit avoidance and self-collision barriers — within the continuous action space.
